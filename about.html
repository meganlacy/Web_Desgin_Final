Amout Me

Hey there, I'm Megan! I live outside Savannah, GA, and I love it here. Since elementary school, I have been creating artwork, but never professionally pursuing it. I have taken some college classes in graphic design, drawing, and 3D art. In 2020 I became a stay at home mom and during my free time I started creating brand logos for fun and learning to code. I enjoy the process of coding because I can build a website from scratch as well as design it. Ultimately this lead me to the decision to pursue a degree in Information Technology at Georgia Southern University.

I am open to learning new skills and finding my way in the tech world. Some of my favorite things include spending time with my children, riding horses, painting, being in nature, the color pink, and relaxing at the beach.
